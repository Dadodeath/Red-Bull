Meet the 16 Roosters of the International Battle Final

Wos (Argentina)

International champion 2018 in the international final played in Argentina, this year he will defend the title in Spain and look for the double-championship, with permission from Azcino and other 14 MCs that will try to prevent it.

Aczino (México)

Considered to be the best freestyler in the world, with a trajectory in Battle of the Roosters that goes back to 2008, this year he arrives at the international final with a desire for vendetta, since last year he fell to Wos in the final in Argentina. Mau el Aczino has become an international star and in 2019 he has started his artistic career. This year he seeks to write a new page in the history of freestyle rap trying to become the first two-time world champion.

Bnet (España)

He discovered the world of freestyle at age 14, at the hands of a colleague, and thanks to the fact that he returned the bug at the age of 17, he began rapping to this day. Without any clear reference to continue in the world of battles, it is clear that what most attracted him to freestyle is rap. He thinks that people cannot be labeled and that each person has characteristics that make him unique.

Jaze (Peru)

His real name is Juan Carlos Iwasaki and he is only 19 years old, but his talent in freestyle began to show him from earlier. When he was a child, MC Mochila, as he is also known, joined a group of friends who practiced improvisation and did cock battles in the parks. As a result, the Alternate Support collective was born in 2017, where Jaze is one of the organizers.

Carpediem (Colombia)

Carpe Diem arrives this year at the international Battle final after wiping out the Red Bull Battle of the Roosters in his country. He got rid of Token in the first round and Airon in the second. He passed a difficult drink to eliminate Marithea, as both starred in a real battalion.

Chang (Venezuela)

In the national final of the Battle of the Roosters Venezuela 2019, known as ‘No chickens are accepted’, the tradition that a champion never repeats triumph is fulfilled again. This time it was Chang who won the victory, putting up with the desire of two-time Letter Championship, which had been crowned in 2018 and started as a clear favorite for the win.

Franco (Uruguay)

Uruguay is back on the world freestyle map with its return to the international final of the Red Bull Battle of the Roosters. In 2019 it comes from Franco, just 13 years after R Loko, the last Uruguayan rooster that participated in an international. The ticket to Spain was won by MC charrúa in the national 3xfreestyle championship.

Jokker (Chile)

It is the Chilean MC with the greatest projection of the moment. Jokker started 2019 sweeping the 420 Bakyard Battle of the Grow Fest, won his Battle regional and also BDM in his hometown. And he signed a spectacular career with second place in the national final of Battle Chile. To achieve this he had to defeat Tom Crowley, who defended the title of Chilean champion of 2018.

Litzen (Perú)

Gustavo Guevara, aka Litzen (Trujillo, Peru) has dominated his regional iron hand in the first year he was enrolled in the Red Bull Battle of the Roosters.

Lobo Estepario (México)

He got rid of Venezuelan Lancer Lirical in the semifinals and defeated Skiper in the final. Lobo Estepario finally collects the witness as the best freestyler in Mexico, after years of absolute domination of the Aczino scene, whose shadow is always elongated

Snk (Costa Rica)

Sergio Guillen, aka SNK (pronounced “that in kei”), was proclaimed champion of 2019 in his fifth participation in the national final of Battle in Costa Rica.

Tito MC (Cuba)

Tito MC arrives in Madrid with well done duties. He was proclaimed champion of the Red Bull Battle of the Roosters Cuba 2019 after eliminating two of the big favorites.

Trueno (Argentina)

Mateo Palacios or, rather, Thunder was crowned with 17 years as Argentina's national champion of the Battle of the Roosters in his first participation In 2019.

Valles-T (Colombia)

The MC of Cali got the direct ticket for the international Battle final 2019 thanks to its third place harvested in Argentina in 2018, year in which it was proclaimed two-time Colombian champion and reached the international final in Argentina as one of the great surprises, although fell to Wos in the semifinals.

Yartzi (USA)

The Red Bull Battle of the Roosters returned to the United States in 2019, after 10 years of absence, with a national final in which we could see the 8 best Spanish-speaking freestylers compete for the title. Born in Ponce, Puerto Rico, Yartzi has been the last to join the list of 16 roosters for the international final.

Zasko Master (España)

His overwhelming victory has caused a real tsunami, since Zasko defines himself, above all, as a rapper. He does not have a pugilistic, falton or violent style, but he wins his opponents with his lyrics and his metrics. 2019 has been his year of consecration and, with a resounding victory in the Spanish national, has shown that whoever follows it gets it.
